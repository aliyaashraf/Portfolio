PROJECTS

#codeADAM
This is a simple tool used to change a phones sound profile. The application has been developed to solve a crucial problem that everyone today faces- misplacing a phone when it is on vibration or silent mode. #codeADAM is the best solution when such an issue arises. This tool can change a missing phones sound profile through any other basic phone- not necessarily a smart phone; by a simple message.
E-serveur
A SaaS project that can replace waiters in hotels or restaurants to take orders from customers. Through this, waiting can be reduced, thus helping customers feel much more satisfied. It can also cut labor costs. Customers can order and pay through this application. The Guide
The Guide deals with all kinds of student information, including admission, academics, hostel and other resource related details. It tracks a students complete information. It also shows all the courses offered by the college along with its fee structure. The project can be easily accessed by every student,faculty, employees of the institution with the aid of his username and password.
